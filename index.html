IntelliFlow

A sophisticated multi-agent data analysis and insights platform

Overview

IntelliFlow orchestrates seven specialized AI agents that collaborate autonomously to extract, process, analyze, and visualize data from diverse sources, transforming raw information into actionable business intelligence with minimal human intervention.

Our Specialized Agents

Data Scout

Discovers and extracts data from various sources including BigQuery, APIs, and databases.

Data Engineer

Transforms raw data into analysis-ready formats through cleaning, normalization, and enrichment.

Analysis Strategist

Determines optimal analytical approaches based on data characteristics and business objectives.

Insight Generator

Applies advanced techniques to discover patterns, trends, and anomalies in processed data.

Visualization Specialist

Creates visual representations of findings through charts, dashboards, and interactive displays.

Narrative Composer

Translates analytical findings into clear, actionable business narratives and recommendations.

Orchestrator

Coordinates the entire agent ecosystem, managing workflows and ensuring seamless collaboration.

Built with Google Cloud and ADK

IntelliFlow leverages the Agent Development Kit (ADK) and Google Cloud services to create a powerful, scalable platform for multi-agent orchestration and data analysis.
